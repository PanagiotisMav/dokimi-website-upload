About Dr. Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a board-certified family medicine physician with a passion for preventive care and patient education. She completed her medical degree at Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine and her residency at Massachusetts General Hospital.

With over 15 years of experience in family medicine, Dr. Johnson has developed expertise in managing chronic conditions, pediatric care, and women's health. She takes pride in creating a welcoming environment where patients feel heard and understood.

Dr. Johnson believes in a holistic approach to healthcare, emphasizing the importance of lifestyle modifications, nutrition, and mental well-being alongside traditional medical treatment.
